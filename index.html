The permanent portal for Swiss open government data goes live on 2 February 2016

The Swiss pilot portal for Open Government Data "opendata.admin.ch" is being replaced by opendata.swiss - going live on February 2, 2016.

In the meantime, it is still possible to access the pilot portal opendata.admin.ch using this temporary link: http://central-ckan.liip.ch.chbar1.nine.ch However, please note that we cannot guarantee that all data is up to date, or that all features are fully functional on this temporary site - as we are fully focused on launching opendata.swiss.

opendata.swiss is a joint project of the Confederation, cantons, communes and other organizations with a mandate from the state. It will make open government data available to the public in a central catalogue. opendata.swiss is operated by the Swiss Federal Archives.

If you have any questions, please contact the opendata.swiss portal team at the Swiss Federal Archives: opendata@bar.admin.ch
