opendata.swiss

The permanent portal for Swiss open government data goes live on 18 January 2016

The Swiss open government data pilot portal "opendata.admin.ch" is being replaced by a new permanent portal: opendata.swiss goes live on 18 January 2016.

opendata.swiss is a joint project of the Confederation, cantons, communes and other organizations with a mandate from the state. It will make open government data available to the public in a central catalogue. opendata.swiss is operated by the Swiss Federal Archives.

If you have any questions, please contact the opendata.swiss portal team at the Swiss Federal Archives: opendata@bar.admin.ch
